About

Turning performance data into tactical clarity.

I am Sam Watts, a multi-sport performance analyst focused on making video and data more useful for coaching, recruitment, and player development.

Background

My experience spans football, basketball, squash, netball, and cricket, working with professional clubs, academies, and national programmes to deliver insight that informs decision-making.

Placements and project work have included Liverpool FC, Cheltenham Town, Bath City FC, Team GB Taekwondo, the RFU, and Cardiff Vikings. Across those settings I have supported match analysis, recruitment work, set-piece review, opponent profiling, and performance reporting.
