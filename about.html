Background

My experience spans football, squash, basketball, taekwondo, hockey, cricket, and rugby, working with professional clubs, academies, universities, and national programmes to deliver insight that informs decision-making.

I currently work through RedZone Analysis in Bath, providing real-time coding and pre- and post-game analysis for environments including PSA Squash and Bath City FC. Project and placement work has also included Liverpool FC U15/U16, Cheltenham Town U18s, Team GB Taekwondo, Hockey Wales, and rugby research support, covering everything from zone penetration and set-piece review to athlete preparation and visual reporting.
